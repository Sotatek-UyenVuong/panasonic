## Dataset Comparison: MELD vs IEMOCAP

| Metric | MELD | IEMOCAP |
|--------|------|----------|
| Vocabulary Size | 10,643 | 3,598 |
| Avg Utterance Length | 8.0 words | 15.8 words |
| Avg Conversation Length | 9.6 utterances | 49.2 utterances |

## Dataset Overview
- Created as extension of EmotionLines dataset
- Contains ~13,000 utterances from 1,433 dialogues
- Source: TV series Friends
- Uses 7 emotion categories: anger, disgust, fear, joy, neutral, sadness, and surprise
